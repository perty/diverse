Islands is an online game of strategy.

Black Elves

Prelude

The sun is shining over the little village and the farmers are plowing. A logger comes out of the woods on his way to the storage bin with his load.

A sudden sound of something big crashing into something even bigger sounds across the sky. The birds stops singing and everyone freezes. A second passes, then they all start waving. "Help!", they mean, "It is the black elves that are coming!".

You have to quickly find an action or the whole population might be swept away by storming black elves. The tar pit, get some tar! Put it in the dry grass!

When the black ones are storming in, you give the command to light a fire in the grass and set them into panic. Those that are not consumed by the fire, retreat to their ship. A few break through but are killed swiftly by brave villagers equipped with shovels.

The villagers cheer as the cracking noise from the ship signals its leaving. The victory was not only a relief but the villagers had also added a new maneuver to their tactics, use fire to stop black elves.

However, the next attack might not be as easy handled. Also, there still may be black elves left on your island and if you do not deal with them, they will deal with you as soon as they have rested long enough.

To search the island efficiently, you need horses, so you command a villager to tame some of the wild ones on the island. Meanwhile, you organize a search party with a few good men.

The horses arrive and you send the search party out. Eventually they find a small group of black elves that is gathering strength for a new attack. Extinguishing them led to another skill for your village, after an attack, send out a party to wipe out remaining enemies.

Islands - an Online Game of Strategy

You village is the sole village on an island that floats in an eternal sea. Once in a while, the island will be attacked by black elves that wish to take you resources and kidnap your people for sacrifice.

It will also happen, that your island will bump into another, similar island. Friend or foe, it can not be known until the very last moment.

As you island develops, it will be come larger in size and your population increase. Also, new technologies and strategies become known, making it possible to handle more difficult situations and stronger foes.

When you log out, life goes on in your village and as you return, things that has passed are to be read in the history book of your island. It may have gone well or it may have gone bad.

Since you can not attend to the island always, the better you can prepare your inhabitants, the better they will do when you are away.

It is your challenge to balance the expansion of your village against the available resources. You must balance a strong military force against a thriving civilian life.

You will form or join groups of islands that become a lot stronger against the attacks from the black elves and other, hostile islands.

Home Page

Every island has a home page on the web. Here the current state of the island and a picture of the island can be seen. Also, you have an avatar on the web and in the game which carries jewelry and other things that express your status.
